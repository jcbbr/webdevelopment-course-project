About me

Hi, I’m Julio Borba a Cybersecurity and Identity & Access Management (IAM) specialist with over a decade of experience helping organizations secure what matters most: their people, data, and infrastructure.

My journey began in Brazil as a Support Analyst, where I cut my teeth monitoring networks and managing incidents. That early exposure sparked my passion for security, which quickly grew into a career focused on protecting complex digital environments. Since 2012, I’ve specialized in IAM and Privileged Access Management, implementing and managing tools like CyberArk, Centrify, Varonis, and SailPoint across global enterprises, such as Mitusbish Bank, Experian, Itau, BNP Paribas, Ajinomoto, Vale among others.

I’ve held roles from hands-on analyst to technical architect and now lead teams as an Information Security Coordinator at SERASA Experian, where I design and scale IAM programs across multiple cloud platforms including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and Alibaba Cloud.

My international experience has taken me from Brazil to Costa Rica and now to Canada, working with multicultural teams and supporting global operations across the Americas, Europe, Asia, and Australia. Along the way, I’ve participated in job rotations, led major migrations, and developed deep expertise in securing access in large-scale environments. Currently based in New Westminster, BC, I’m also pursuing a Diploma in Computing Studies and Information Systems at Douglas College to continue evolving alongside the rapidly shifting cybersecurity landscape. I’m fluent in Portuguese, English, and Spanish, with intermediate French, and I bring not just technical skill but a passion for building secure, scalable systems that empower people to work safely and confidently.


The Road Ahead

As digital identities become more critical and threat landscapes more complex, my mission is to stay at the forefront—whether by leading IAM strategies, advising on secure cloud adoption, or shaping the next generation of cybersecurity architecture.
